Family Photo & Immich Project: Preserving Memories

In this digital age, it's more important than ever to preserve our precious family memories. This project aims to digitize my collection of family photos and set up Immich as a secure and accessible storage solution. By doing so, I hope to:

  • Protect my (and others) photos from physical damage and deterioration.
  • Make them easily accessible to share with family and friends.
  • Create a lasting digital archive for future generations.
  • Improved Privacy sith a selfhosted Immich setup
  • Basically unlimited storage for free
