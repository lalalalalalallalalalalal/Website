Who am I

Hey there! My name is Pétur Ingi Bache. I'm a half-English, half-Icelandic tech enthusiast living in Vesturbærinn. Born on July 28th, 2011, my curiosity about how things work and the thrill of making something from scratch fuels my passion for all things tech, especially anything related to manufacturing!

A Blend of Backgrounds

Growing up with an Icelandic mom and an English dad has given me a unique perspective. I'm fluent in both languages and enjoy the cultural richness it brings.

Building and Creating

I'm fueled by a passion for taking an idea and turning it into reality. This fascination with the entire creation process starts with sketching and brainstorming. I then use powerful CAD software like Solidworks to design my creations in detail. Once the design is finalized, I can use 3D printing or other manufacturing techniques to bring my ideas to life. This blend of design and manufacturing is what excites me the most!

Exploring Manufacturing

I'm fascinated by the different processes involved in manufacturing, from using tools like lathes to understanding how complex machines work together on a production line. While Solidworks is a powerful CAD software I use to design these creations, the actual 3D printing or manufacturing process itself excites me. Whether it's using 3D printing technology or traditional methods, I find the idea of creating something in bulk, efficient, and precise truly exciting. I'm also thinking about looking into CNC machines (Computer Numerical Control) to learn more about how they can be used in the manufacturing process. CNC machines allow for precise control over tools and can create complex parts, which seems like a perfect fit for my interest in design and manufacturing.

Beyond the Tech

While I love tech, I also enjoy learning new things outside the digital realm. I'm a hands-on learner who enjoys working with various materials and tools. This includes using a lathe to create precise shapes, working with wood for carpentry projects, and even experimenting with silicone and its different applications.. I find exploring how things function in the physical world fascinating, whether it's working with wood, using a lathe to create precise shapes, or understanding the properties of silicone and its applications.

A Touch of Quirky

Let's face it, everyone has their quirks. In my case, it happens to be a love for the cartoon "Bluey" (hey it's a great show!), and I also love cables! There's something strangely satisfying about cables. I just love the look of them, all wound up together. Maybe it's the different colors, or the way they can be neatly organized. Whatever it is, I find them fascinating. And of course, I can't forget about my fascination with bald men, especially a certain fellow named Hörður. Don't ask, it's a long story (and maybe a little embarrassing), but it's a part of who I am!

That's Me!

That's a glimpse into who I am: a curious and creative kid from Iceland with a blend of English and Icelandic heritage, and a passion for exploring the world of technology and manufacturing.
